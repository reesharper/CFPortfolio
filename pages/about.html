About Page

I chose Lambda because I wanted to find a way into the tech industry. I kept searching for the right role to play in the industry bouncing from sound production to videography to video editing in different jobs and volenteer rolls, yet none of them gave me excitement as a career. Then my cousin who had been studying computer science at Belmont University proposed the idea of Lambda School. I had been out of school for a year deciding whether to go back and he was looking for a different program to pursue his passion for computer science. We pondered the idea for a while, then COVID-19 hit. This solidified our decision, picking a 9 month program that is built to be online costing a fraction of the price over a 4 year university that is scrambling to make online its primary learning structure was a non starter. Lambda it is!
